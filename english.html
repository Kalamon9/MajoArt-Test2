About

Paweł Majchrzak, pseudonym Maja, automotive fan with an artistic soul. He believes that art should be available to everyone.

Paweł's paintings are inspired by the beauty of women and nature. Paweł likes to use strong colors, in saturated reds in contrast to blues. Children are also often subject of his works.

Paweł's works provoke the viewer and arouse the interest of many art lovers.

FAQ

1. Is it possible to request a painting?

Yes, please contact us by calling or emailing to discuss the details.

2. What is the price of a work?

The price depends on the material and size of the work. You can expect prices in the range from PLN 200 to PLN 500. The preferred method of payment is bank transfer.

3. What about shipping and delivery when buying art online?

One of our main priorities is to make sure your artwork arrives safely and quickly. The delivery times may vary depending on the final destination.

4. What if I don’t like the work? Or what if it arrived damaged?

If you do not like the work upon receiving it, you have up to 14 days to return the work starting from the day it’s delivered.

In the event that it arrives damaged, you can refuse the parcel and therefore not be held responsible.
